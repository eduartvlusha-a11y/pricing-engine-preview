# 🧠 Mandate Intelligence — Pricing Engine

**A deterministic real estate pricing system that transforms pricing decisions into structured, data-backed strategies.**

---

## 🚀 Overview

Mandate Intelligence is designed for brokers and real estate agencies who want to move beyond subjective pricing decisions.

Instead of guessing the “right price”, the system defines **market positioning**, evaluates **risk**, and projects **financial impact** using a structured decision framework.

It replaces intuition with clarity, making pricing decisions explainable, consistent, and defensible.

---

## ⚙️ What This System Does

- Transforms pricing into a **strategic positioning problem**
- Quantifies **risk, probability, and value erosion**
- Provides **clear justification** for client conversations
- Enables structured **decision-making under market pressure**

---

## 🧩 Core Capabilities

- Deterministic pricing engine (no AI randomness)  
- Seller vs Strategic Entry pricing comparison  
- Correction probability modeling (30 / 60 / 90 days)  
- Value erosion projections  
- Negotiation leverage analysis  
- Time pressure risk evaluation  
- Scenario-based decision dashboard  

---

## 📊 How It Works

### Input

- Estimated Market Value (EMV)  
- Seller Asking Price  
- Market Conditions  

---

### Output

- Recommended pricing strategy  
- Risk level and correction probability  
- Financial impact (value erosion)  
- Strategic positioning insights
